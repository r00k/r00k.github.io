About Me

After a successful career building tech companies, I've shifted my focus to local politics and community advocacy in Somerville. Currently, I'm running The Somerville Beacon, a blog dedicated to covering important local issues that affect our community.

In my previous chapter, I founded and led several successful tech companies, including Tuple, a remote pair programming tool. I also created Refactoring Rails and co-created Upcase, Trailmix, and Briefs.

I'm passionate about sharing knowledge and building community. You can find me hosting the Experimental Podcast, writing on my blog, or speaking at conferences about both tech entrepreneurship and local governance.
