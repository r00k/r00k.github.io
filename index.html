About Me

I spent around a dozen years trying to get really good at programming. That journey culminated in co-founding Tuple, a remote pair programming tool, which went really well thanks to some good fortune. Now, I've shifted my focus to local politics and community advocacy in Somerville, where I run The Somerville Beacon.

Throughout my tech career, I've created several successful projects including Refactoring Rails and co-created Upcase, Trailmix, and Briefs. The technical skills I developed have given me a strong analytical approach to my current work.

I'm passionate about sharing knowledge and building community. You can find me hosting the Experimental Podcast, writing on my blog, or speaking at conferences about both tech entrepreneurship and local governance.
